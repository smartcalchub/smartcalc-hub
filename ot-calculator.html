Use this calculator to estimate overtime pay based on monthly salary, working days, working hours, OT hours and OT multiplier.

What is Overtime Pay?

Overtime pay is extra payment for work performed beyond normal working hours. The exact legal formula depends on country, employment law and company policy.

加班费是员工在正常工作时间以外工作所获得的额外工资。实际算法会根据国家、劳动法和公司政策不同而变化。

Formula / 公式

Hourly Rate = Monthly Salary ÷ Working Days ÷ Working Hours Per Day
OT Pay = Hourly Rate × OT Hours × OT Multiplier

Example / 例子

Monthly Salary: 3,000
Working Days: 26
Working Hours: 8
OT Hours: 10
OT Multiplier: 1.5x

Hourly Rate = 3000 ÷ 26 ÷ 8 = 14.42

OT Pay = 14.42 × 10 × 1.5 = 216.35

FAQ

Is this calculator suitable worldwide?
Yes, because the OT multiplier and working days are customizable. However, always check your local employment law.

What does 1.5x OT mean?
It means the employee receives 150% of the normal hourly wage.

Can I use this for Singapore or Malaysia?
Yes, but the result is an estimate. Please confirm with official employment rules or HR policy.
