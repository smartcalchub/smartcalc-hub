📏 CBM Calculator / CBM 体积计算器

Calculate shipment volume in cubic meters.

What is CBM?

CBM means cubic meter, used in shipping and warehouse planning.

Formula

CBM = Length × Width × Height

Example

400 × 320 × 210 mm = 0.02688 CBM.

FAQ

CBM is used for sea freight, storage and cargo planning.

⚖️ BMI Calculator / BMI 计算器

Calculate Body Mass Index.

What is BMI?

BMI estimates whether your weight is healthy for your height.

Formula

BMI = Weight(kg) ÷ Height²(m)

Category

<18.5 Underweight, 18.5–24.9 Normal, 25–29.9 Overweight, 30+ Obese.

FAQ

BMI is a general reference and may not suit athletes with high muscle mass.
